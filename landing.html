Golf Ball Launcher

This was my first "engineering" class project where we competed with other groups in our class and Community colleges around us. The goal was to launch a ball accurately. The target was an hoola hoop with a 5 gallon bucket at the center. You got 5 points for luanching it inside the bucket, 3 points for hitting the bucket, and 1 pont for inside the hoola-hoop.

Our team scored the most points out of 3 shots and won the competition.
